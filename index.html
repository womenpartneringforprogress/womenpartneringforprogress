Women Partnering for Progress

Everyone does better when women do better. Women have diverse and valuable experiences. Women in leadership are catalyst for change. Women achieve greater change when they work together as their authentic selves. However, women are underrepresented and underdeveloped in leadership roles. Civic involvement is not accessible to women in Indiana. Women are often left out of critical public policy discussions.

We are starting with training and mentoring of other women. Through this program, you'll meet other motivated women who want to impact the areas where they work and live. Sessions will develop professional skills and discuss how to achieve career goals. We will begin to encourage collaboration between sectors and leaders to improve the quality of our institutions by adopting and strengthening policies that empower women.

Our primary goal is to create progressive change in our communities by connecting young women leaders.

We are women who unabashedly support other women. We believe in progressive ideas and are driven and motivated to make a difference. We take leadership seriously and contribute to conversations in our own unique and valuable way. Professionals aged 22-35 and living in the Indianapolis area, we are Women Partnering for Progress.
