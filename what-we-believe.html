Our Mission

We are women who unabashedly support other women. We believe in progressive ideas and are driven and motivated to make a difference. We take leadership seriously and contribute to conversations in our own unique and valuable way. Our members tend to be young professionals aged ~22-35 and living in the Indianapolis area.


Women Partnering for Progress was started in May 2016 in the midst of an election cycle that was divisive and harmful for women. Our founders were brought together by the shared frustration with the stagnation of progressive policy in Indiana and the inaccessibility of politics to young women. They soon realized that they weren't the only ones who felt this way and realized that women training other women is a powerful proposition. Since forming, WPP has convened peer-to-peer dialogs on policy topics and critical skills. We can't wait to build on this early success with our first class of Fellows in the Fall of 2017.
